Data Visualization Homework #1 - Tableau

The purpose of this homework is to explore Tableau and use it to plot visualizations based on a data set of choice.

ABOUT THE DATASET:


The dataset depicts information about the sales on Black Friday and is taken from Kaggle. It conveys the following information:
Gender of customer
Age group of the customer
State the customer lives in
Marital status of the customer
The product ID of the items the customer bought
The amount the customer spent
The following sections display the questions asked of the dataset along with the visualization of it.

Question 1.


QUESTION: What was the sale in the three different states on Black Friday?


INFORMATION DERIVED: We see a symbol view which marks the values of the amount of money spent in each state. This is depicted using plus symbols.

Question 2.


QUESTION: How do people of different gender, age group and marital status spend their money in the three states?


INFORMATION DERIVED: We see a horizontal bar view which show us the amount of money spent in each state. The bars are plotted seperately depending on which gender, age group and the marital status the customer belongs to.

Question 3.


QUESTION: What was the division of the sold items in the three states?


INFORMATION DERIVED: We see a packed bubble view which shows us the distribution of the sale of items by their IDs in the different states.

CONCLUSION:


The main aim of picking this dataset for visualization was to help a user understand the sale patterns on Black Friday. The visualizations would help the user figure out patterns and help him/her in analyzing the data easily. The following trends were observed on the basis of the visualization:
The amount of money spent in different states in comparison to each other.
The different types of target audiences in the states and the usual spending habits of the different types of people.
Which items are more popular in which state and are generally bought in relation to each other.
These types of visualizations can be used by shops to figure out which products to stock up on and how to market them according to people's needs and will help boost their sales.
